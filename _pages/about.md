I obtained my bachelor degree in Software Engineering at Tongji University, Shanghai, advised by [Prof. Lin Zhang](http://sse.tongji.edu.cn/linzhang/). During my undergraduate study, my research focused on pervasive and trustworthy systems design and implementation using machine learning methods. After graduation, I interned at Smart City Group of SenseTime, working on improving neural network efficiency with Neural Architecture Search. Currently, I am pursuing my PhD degree in computer science at Emory University.

Research Interest
======
Broadly, my research interests revolve around machine learning. Currently, I am working on applying machine learning to Brain Imaging, advised by [Prof. Ying Guo](https://www.yingguo.us/home).
